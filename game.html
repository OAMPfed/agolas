AGOLAS

AGOLAS is a 'Game of Thrones' inspired Snakes and Ladder type boardgame. The game is simple. Align yourself with a house and become their champion in the ultimate quest to become the ruling faction of the known world. Roll the dice and risk it all. Winnings are coming.

The Rules

  • The player rolls a dice once per turn. If a roll results in 6, another roll is automatically done.
  • There are hidden traps that will trigger random events resulting in penalties.
  • The winner is the first player to land exactly on tile 30. Rolls that move past the final tile will be subtracted and the player will move backwards until a roll lands on tile 30.
